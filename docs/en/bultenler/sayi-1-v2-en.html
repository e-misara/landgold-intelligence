Methodology

Consistency formula

Score = (topic_match × 0.4 + numeric_density × 0.4 + tradia_verification × 0.2) × 100
  • topic_match: Jaccard similarity of YouTube and written content per overlap month (13 core economic topics)
  • numeric_density: Full-text ratio × 0.4 + 0.4 (fixed baseline)
  • tradia_verification: Alignment of analyst's specific forecasts with CBRT KFE + Expectations Survey + TÜİK (0-1)

Data sources

  • ▸ YouTube transcripts: 3,001 videos (CC-Social Phase 1)
  • ▸ Written: Mahfi blog (Atom RSS), Paraborsa (Cloudscraper), Sabah (HTML scrape)
  • ▸ Verification: CBRT data (2026-05), TÜİK, Tradia news pool

Transparency
